<h3>Foxy: 3D Calculator (individual project)</h3>
<a href="https://apps.apple.com/us/app/foxy-3d-calculator/id6740338165?mt=12">Foxy: 3D Calculator</a> is a macOS app for visualizing 3D functions published on the AppStore. Whether you're a math enthusiast, a student, or just someone who loves tinkering with graphs, Foxy lets you plot functions of two variables with stunning clarity. Start with customizable inputs or use a variety of built-in templates, including quadratic, trigonometric, logarithmic, and more!
